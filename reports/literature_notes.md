## Notes on Academic Rigour for COMP3931

Per the Final Report Guidance:
- Background chapter should **critically review** prior work, not just summarise it
- For each paper: what did they do? what were the limitations? how does YOUR work differ?
- Aim for **15–20 papers** minimum, with at least 8–10 being directly relevant to rugby/sport ML
- Peer-reviewed journals and conference proceedings (e.g., IJCAI, AAAI, KDD) carry more weight than blog posts or arXiv preprints (though preprints are acceptable if clearly labelled)
